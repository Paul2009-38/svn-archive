You'll need Processing 0090 or higher.  www.processing.org

This uses a few bits of openmap (the UTMPoint class and it's dependencies) and the Apache XML-RPC and Commons Codecs libraries.

Copy the "utm" folder into your Processing sketchbook, put your own username and password in and hit run.
